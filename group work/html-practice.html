Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is a cute, proud, and stubborn breed of dog. They are a hunting dog originating in Japan and are currently the number one most beloved dog breed there. Although the Shiba Inu is prized for its beautiful foxlike appearance and thick orange to tan coat, they come in other colors such as black, cream and sesame.

Chow Chow

The Chow Chow is a dog breed originally from northern China. The Chow Chow is a sturdily built dog, square in profile, with a broad skull and small, triangular, erect ears with rounded tips. The breed is known for a very dense double coat that is either smooth or rough. For more information visit The American Kennel Club.
